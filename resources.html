Resources

RebootEarth resources are designed to empower youth-led innovation. Whether you're launching a circular startup, designing a green product, or leading a school campaign, these tools help you go further, faster.

Our startup toolkit includes pitch deck templates, business model canvases, and impact tracking sheets. These resources help you plan, present, and grow your venture with clarity and confidence.

Design guides focus on circularity—covering lifecycle thinking, modular design, and sustainable material selection. They help you build products that regenerate, not deplete.

Funding and competition links connect you to youth innovation challenges, grant platforms, and accelerator programs. We spotlight opportunities that align with purpose and impact.

Our learning library features curated articles, videos, and case studies on circular economy and green entrepreneurship. It’s a space for deep dives, inspiration, and shared knowledge.

All resources are open-access and youth-centered. We believe in collaboration, transparency, and co-creation. If you have a resource to share, reach out—we’re always expanding.
